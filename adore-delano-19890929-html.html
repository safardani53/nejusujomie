Daniel Anthony Noriega, better known by the stage name Adore Delano, is an American drag queen, singer-songwriter, and television personality. Noriega appeared as a contender in American Idol's seventh season in 2008, then competed as Adore Delano in season six of RuPaul's Drag Race, placing in the top three overall. Delano went on to star in the second season of RuPaul's Drag Race All Stars, finishing 9th after voluntarily leaving the competition. In June 2019, a New York magazine jury ranked her sixth on the list of "America's strongest drag queens," the ranking of the 100 former Drag Race competitors. Delano has recorded and released three studio albums: Till Death Do Us Party, After Party, and Anything.
