Zoey Deutch and Regé-Jean Page visit Audi Activesphere Concept in Aspen during the Audi FIS Ski World Cup Celebration on March 04, 2023 in Aspen, Colorado.

Audi presented the U.S. debut of the 2023 Audi activesphere concept vehicle at the Audi FIS Ski World Cup in Aspen, CO on March 3 and 4th. The German-based company hosted a weekend-long event with top executives and global leaders including Marc Lichte, Head of Audi Design, Daniel Weissland, Head of Audi North America, Tara Rush, CMO, Audi of America and Emilie Cotter, Chief Communications Officer of Audi of America were also in attendance to unveil the concept vehicle to U.S. audiences.

Later in the evening, the automobile company partnered with Casa Tua for a private dining experience unlike any other. The restaurants exceptionally cozy, sophisticated spaces with reclaimed-wood details were a perfect match for the boundary-crossing electric Audi activesphere concept vehicle revealed during the weekend.
