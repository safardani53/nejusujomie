Bam Adebayo Stays Mum About Having A Girlfriend Or Wife

July 18, 2024
blog

American professional basketball player Bam Adebayo has been actively involved in the sport for nearly a decade, which has made his fans curious about his personal life, especially his dating life.

To address the curiosity of fans, Adebayo does not seem to be romantically involved with anyone. He is a very private person, and he has not shared any glimpses of his dating life in public.

However, rumors were claiming that Adebayo had secretly tied the knot; but these speculations were later proven to be baseless and fake. There is not a single piece of evidence supporting any claims of his marriage.

Even a thorough examination of his Instagram profile reveals no pictures indicating that he is in a relationship. The majority of the content on his feed is related to his career, demonstrating his current focus on his NBA journey.

Adebayo is currently committed to developing his basketball skills and is directing all his attention toward becoming an NBA star.

Bam Adebayo’s dating history

Similar to Adebayo’s current dating status, there is no recorded history of him being in a public relationship. He has also not been linked to any well-known personality to this date.

However, in 2021, the basketball player was spotted watching a game with three girls who appeared to be quite comfortable around him. Despite them enjoying each other’s company, fans found a way to playfully tease him and his companions.

One person tweeted, “Shiiit, no wonder he’s been playing like a man possessed…ol girl is a slump buster that’ll last a career right there.”

Similarly, another said, “Man ok, on the surface this is funny AF! Maybe I’m getting soft in my old age, but damn I feel bad for that poor girl! Everyone is roasting him but primarily at her expense! I’d be so pissed if that was my sister or daughter getting dragged through the mud to trust him.”

Amid these comments, some individuals stepped forward to defend the girls and criticize those who engaged in making fun of people’s appearances. Despite the widespread online discussions about Adebayo being seen with girls, he chose to remain silent on the situation.

Inside Bam Adebayo’s family background

Adebayo was born in Newark, New Jersey, to a Nigerian Yoruba father, John Adebayo, and an African-American mother, Marilyn Blout.

As a child, his mother gave him the nickname Bam Bam after a memorable incident at the age of one when he flipped over a coffee table while watching The Flintstones, resembling the show’s character Bamm-Bamm Rubble.

After his parents’ separation, his mother raised him in Newark. Later, when he turned seven, the NBA player moved with his mother to North Carolina, marking a significant chapter in his life. Unfortunately, this relocation resulted in limited interactions with his father.

The physical distance and lack of connection with his father during his formative years led Adebayo to harbor resentment toward his last name and Nigerian heritage.

Later, when Adebayo reached the age of 16, he actively embarked on a journey to learn more about his Nigerian background. Over time, he has not only reconciled with his heritage but has actively chosen to celebrate and incorporate it into his identity.

However, tragically, the basketball player’s father passed away in 2020 while peacefully residing in Nigeria. Now, he is left with the love and support of his mother alone.

Adebayo has openly shared numerous special moments with his mother on his Instagram, showcasing the close bond and affection they share. On February 20, 2020, he shared a heartfelt picture where he stood beside his mother, who was proudly holding his trophy.

Alongside the post, he captioned, “The bond between mother and son is a special one. It remains unchanged by time or distance. It is the purest love—unconditional and true. It’s understanding of any situation and forgiving of any mistake.”

Similarly, on December 7, 2021, the NBA player shared a delightful picture featuring him and his mother relaxing on a boat. Accompanying the post was a touching caption, “Sons may turn into men, and grow out of their toys, but in the hearts of their mothers, they are still little boys.”

He continued, “Happy Birthday to the woman that made me… me! You’re still the greatest I’ve ever seen do this sh*t called life.”

Upon seeing the post, numerous fans and followers flooded the comments section with expressions of admiration for the heartwarming bond between the NBA player and his mother.

One person expressed, “So cute!!!! As a mommy, I melt seeing the way you treat her. As a daughter, I miss more than everything having these moments with my amazing mommy!’”

Brief on Bam Adebayo’s career

Adebayo displayed a keen interest in basketball from an early age. His journey in the professional realm began on June 22, 2017, when he was selected with the 14th overall pick in the 2017 NBA draft by the Miami Heat.

The following year saw notable highlights in his career, including a standout performance with a career-high 21 rebounds and 16 points in a 125–115 loss against the Toronto Raptors. Further showcasing his skills, he recorded 22 points and 10 rebounds in a 115–98 win over the Phoenix Suns.

Likewise, on December 10, 2019, the NBA player achieved a significant milestone by recording his first career triple-double with a career-high 30 points, 11 rebounds, and 11 assists in a thrilling 135–121 overtime win against the Atlanta Hawks.

Bam Adebayo has harbored a passion for basketball since his childhood. (Source: Instagram)

In the same month, Adebayo earned the title of Eastern Conference Player of the Week for the games played from December 9 to 15. During this period, he averaged an impressive 20.0 points, 11.3 rebounds, and 8.7 assists per game.

In 2022, the basketball player continued to shine as he tied his season high with 38 points on 12-of-16 shooting from the field and a perfect 14-of-14 from the free-throw line in a 116–111 win over the Indiana Pacers.

The momentum carried into November 6, 2023, when Adebayo delivered another outstanding performance, securing a triple-double with 22 points, 19 rebounds, and 10 assists, accompanied by two steals and two blocks in a thrilling 108–107 victory over the Los Angeles Lakers.
