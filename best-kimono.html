Kimonos are the perfect garment to pair with your summer outfits. They can easily change the entire look of your simple outfit and make you look fancy and elegant.

If you want a fashionable look for summer, then you can use this buying guide to the chicest kimonos in 2023 to make a good purchase.

Factors to Consider When Purchasing a Kimono

Let's look at the factors you should keep in mind while purchasing a kimono in 2023.

Authenticity

The first thing you should keep in mind while purchasing a kimono is whether you are looking for an authentic traditional Japanese kimono or a westernized kimono. This will give you direction and help you find the specific type of kimono you are looking for.

Purpose

The next thing you should keep in mind while searching for a kimono is the purpose you are purchasing it for. Figure out whether you are purchasing it for a play or to represent Japanese culture. You can also purchase a kimono to wear to informal events because they are very popular in fashion nowadays.

Measurements

The next thing you need to make sure of is your body type and body measurement. Make sure that you purchase a kimono according to your size, and take your specific body measurements for a traditional Japanese kimono. This will help you out in purchasing the perfect size for your body, and it is sure to end up looking great on you as well. But if you are looking for something informal, then the size and measurement might not matter, because loose and flowy kimonos are also available on the market.

Gender expression

Before purchasing a kimono, it is important to consider the gender you will express with the kimono. Kimonos are available for males and females, and both of them are worn differently. There are different sizing rules for both males and females. The masculine kimono type is easier to wear and maintain as compared to the woman's style.

Material

The next thing you should make sure of is the material that you prefer, as well as the fabric quality. Figure out if you are looking for a kimono made of cotton, polyester, silk, denim, or any other fabric. Generally, silk kimonos are the preferred choice of most people, but you can choose any material that you like.
