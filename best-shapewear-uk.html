Which company has the best shapewear?

There are so many shapewear brands available in the UK in 2023 that finding the right garments for you can be tricky. The most popular luxury shapewear brands on the market are undoubtably Skims (owned by Kim Kardashian) and Yitty (Lizzo's brand with Fabletics), and part of this is down to their products going viral on social media time and time again. 

The best shapewear company for you totally depends on your budget and preferences. There are more affordable options out there at brands like M&S, Pour Moi, and Everybody at Very. 

Does shapewear flatten stomachs?

Yes, shapewear is capable of creating the appearance of a flatter stomach. Certain garments, like shaping shorts, briefs, knickers and bodysuits, will specifically target your stomach with elastic and rigid fabric that artificially holds in your stomach. 

Which type of shapewear is best?

There is not a 'best' type of shapewear. The best shapewear for you depends on the area you want to target and what you're planning to wear it underneath. For example, a all-in-one unitard wouldn't work with a strapless mini dress because you'd see the straps. Equally, if you're fair skinned and wearing a nude or partially see-through dress, you should choose shapewear in a colour close to your skin tone rather than a black or brown garment. 

You may need to own several pairs of shapewear for different purposes.

What are the different kinds of shapewear?

Thread's senior stylist, Millie Rich says that there are so many different types to wear depending on what you want to achieve.

"Controlling camisoles - perfect to help control your tummy; controlling tights/shorts - can be worn under dresses and skirts to shape your bum and thighs; corsets - on-trend garments used to make your waist appear smaller while shaping your tummy," she explains. "Body shapers - a full-coverage shaper that shapes your torso and your breasts; control pants - a great option for cinching your tummy and streamlining your waist; salf slips - perfect if you want to wear a mini dress or skirt, they shape your waist, tummy, and thighs; dress slips - like a half slip, a dress slip is great to wear underneath a mini dress and provides full support from the bust to the hips."

Why does shapewear roll down?

Rich says that the best way to stop shapewear from rolling down is to shop for the correct size. Pretty straightforward.

Can you wear shapewear everyday?

"There's no hard rules when it comes to wearing shapewear every day, but there are some things you should be aware of," says Rich. "There are different types of control levels when it comes to shapewear, but light control is recommended if you want to wear shapewear every day, as it is much more stretchy and flexible. Firm-control, on the other hand, is often more rigid and is best kept for special occasions, as it offers a lot of sculpting, but not much comfort.

"For the best breast support it is best to wear underwire bikini tops and bikinis with a push-up quality, which can help make exercise, such as swimming, more comfortable. Simply Be blends theirs with high elastane, nylon fabrics, and thicker straps, so you feel comfortable and supported all over."

Does shapewear really work?

Since shapewear is a compression garment, it can smooth out lumps and bumps, accentuate curves and make whole areas appear thinner and flatter.

Shapewear is designed to make everything look smooth and is worn underneath clothes, such as dresses and tight trousers, but it won't create permanent changes.

Some people insist that wearing waist trainers can shrink your waist over time, but there's no evidence to prove that.

There is, however, scientific evidence to show wearing too-tight garments for too long can create digestive problems.

How do I choose shapewear?

The type of shapewear you choose depends on the look you're trying to achieve - and the occasion you need it for.

Be sure to pick the right size (the size you normally wear). Tone your legs with shapewear shorts or leggings, high-waist knickers can do wonders for making tummies look flat, and a shapewear bodysuit will smooth the whole torso (that's why it's a favourite under bodycon dresses).

How much do body shapers cost?

As with any item of clothing, you'll find body shapers at a range of price points.

M&S is a good option for budget-friendly shapewear, with 2-pack knickers starting at £10.

Premium shapewear brands like Spanx are more expensive: you're looking at paying around £60 for the Suit Your Fancy high-rise stretch jersey thong.

Should you buy shapewear a size smaller?

No, you should buy shapewear in your regular size.

Since shapewear is made from slimming, stretch fabrics, it will fit snugly over your body when you get the right size - that's how it was designed to work.

What is the best shapewear for under a dress?
