Parenthood looks good on them! Corey Stoll and his actress wife, Nadia Bowers, have welcomed their first child together.

Earlier this month, Bowers, 42, revealed the news herself in a sweet Facebook photo of the couple kissing. Commenters quickly pointed out that the House of Cards and The Strain star Stoll, 39, was wearing a baby carrier. Their little one's arm was also noticeable.

The couple announced in June that Bowers was pregnant. She debuted her growing baby bump at the 2015 Tony Awards. Back in June, Stoll gushed about becoming a first-time dad. 

"I'm so excited. I'm sure there's too much to be nervous about to actually really be nervous about anything specific," he said during an interview on HuffPost Live at the time. "I just sort of figure, there are people a lot dumber than me who have done it, and kept that baby alive, so I think we'll be okay. Whatever I mess up, she's a lot more competent than me in many ways, so I think the kid will be okay."

Stoll and Bowers wed this past June.
