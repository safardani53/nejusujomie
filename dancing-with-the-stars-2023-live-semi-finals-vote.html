Inside Xochitl Gomez's rise to fame

After her breakthrough role on the Disney Channel, Xochitl Gomez was cast as Chucky in the 2019 film Shadow Wolves.

The same year, Xochitl appeared in an episode of You're the Worst.

She starred in a 2020 episode of Gentefied before landing her first central cast role as Dawn Schafer on The Baby-Sitters Club, a Netflix series.

Xochitl played Francis in the 2021 short film, Boob Sweat.

Her most prominent role came with her introduction into the Marvel Cinematic Universe as America Chavez in Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness.

What to know about Xochitl Gomez

Born on April 29, 2006, Xochitl Gomez is a native of Los Angeles, California.

Her first name is the Nahuatl word for flower.

At the age of five, Xochitl began acting in local plays and musicals.

She made her first on-screen appearance in 2018 as an extra on Raven's Home, the spin-off series of Disney's That's So Raven.

Xochitl is heading to next week's live finale for Dancing with the Stars.
