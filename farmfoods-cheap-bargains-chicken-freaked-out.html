A SAVVY shopper has shared the seven best bargains he spotted in Farmfoods this week.

But Elliot William, who regularly shares his handy tips and tricks to make the cost of living crisis more manageable, was met with mixed opinions on his online clip.

When in Farmfoods, Elliot found a whole chicken for £1.99, tuna for just 49p and ice creams super cheap.

However, people were ‘freaked out’ at the prices and said Farmfoods was like going back in time. 

Farmfoods is a British frozen food and grocery supermarket chain based in Cumbernauld, Scotland. 

There are over 300 shops in the United Kingdom, of which more than a hundred are in Scotland.
