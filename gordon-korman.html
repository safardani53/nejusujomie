Who is Gordon Korman:

Gordon Korman is a famous Children’s Author. He was born on October 23, 1963 and his birthplace is Montreal, Canada. Gordon is also well known as, Author of more than sixty children’s books, including the Dive series, the Swindle series, and the Monday Night Football Club series. His non-serial books include A Semester in the Life of a Garbage Bag, The Twinkie Squad, and No More Dead Dogs.

Gordon is originated from Canada. Robert Munsch was another popular children’s book author from Canada.
