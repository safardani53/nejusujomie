Who is Harry Hill's wife Magda Archer?

Magda Archer is married to TV funny man Harry Hill.

The pair have three children together, Kitty Clover, Winifred Millicent and Frederica Aster.

However, Harry previously said that he regretted working so much as his children grew up.

He said: "I went through a phase where I did too much work. Particularly when I was doing TV Burp, I was physically there but very distracted."

When did Harry Hill marry Magda Archer?

Harry and Magda tied the knot in 1996, in Wandsworth, London, and have been together ever since.

The family now live in Whitstable, Kent.

Harry has said in the past that his wife Magda is the love of his life.

Is Magda Archer an artist?

Magda is an artist.

She trained at Ravensbourne College of Art, Chelsea School of Art, and the Royal College of Art before producing a series of group exhibitions.

Working alongside her husband, she was the co-writer and illustrator for the Harry Hill Fun Book.

Some of the artwork produced for the book appeared in Peter Blake About Collage at the Liverpool Tate in 2000.

Magda has written and illustrated a children’s book, Watch Out Arthur! published by Harper Collins.

In 2011 Magda had her first solo show, Crazy Mad at the Cornerhouse, Manchester.

In 2012 Magda's paintings were used by Comme des Garcons for their spring/summer Shirt Campaign.

Her work can be purchased from Jealous Gallery where prices range from £35 to £550.

Magda's artwork tends to include brightly coloured objects and animals alongside witty sayings.

Fellow artist Grayson Perry has declared on social media in the past that Magda is one of his favourite artists.
