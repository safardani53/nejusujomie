Jaimie Alexander’s See-Through Dress For ‘Thor’ Premiere:

The star looked cool and confident despite the fact that she was showing off so much skin beneath the revealing ensemble. Jaimie kept the styling simple and her skin was definitely her best accessory on this occasion, although she added a pair of dangling statement earrings, kohl-rimmed eyes, and a nude lip to the glam get-up.

While Jaimie definitely took the trend to an entire new level, it did remind us of the now infamous dress Gwyneth Paltrow wore to the Iron Man 3 premiere. Considering the uproar Gwyn caused by revealing the outline of her backside, we can only imagine what critics will have to say of Jaimie’s dress!

Sure, Jaimie’s dress had long sleeves and extended past her ankles, but it was far from modest — especially because the entire middle of the dress was sheer! In the back the exposed zipper was visible and it stopped right above her butt, while a small piece of fabric provided some coverage for the star. In the front it was just as scandalous, with the sheer detailing extending below her tan lines. This dress makes Gwyneth look like she was dressed for Sunday dinner!

Sheer Dresses Rule The Red Carpet:

While sheer is one of the sexiest trends on the red carpet right now, with everyone from Nicole Richie to Victoria Beckham rocking the trend, Jaimie’s dress definitely takes the cake! In fact, it’s one of the most daring dresses that has ever displayed the trend.
