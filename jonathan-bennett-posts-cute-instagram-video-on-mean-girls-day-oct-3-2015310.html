Jonathan Bennett Posts Cute Instagram Video on Mean Girls Day, Oct. 3

August 16, 2024
blog

Grool! Jonathan Bennett shared an adorable video on Instagram on Saturday, Oct. 3, in honor of Mean Girls Day.

The actor, 34, played the seriously cute Aaron Samuels in the 2004 hit film, and in one now-classic scene, asks Lindsay Lohan’s character, Cady Heron, what day it was — Oct. 3!

In the video, Bennett seems to have just woken up. “Uh, I have a question. Does anybody know what day it is?” he coyly asks, batting his eyelashes.

Lohan’s love interest in the film first took to Twitter last night, tweeting, “Ok ok! My phone is blowing up so let’s make this a team effort. First let me clear my throat… eh hem…. so…. what day is today?”

And on Saturday morning, he tweeted, “It’s a very important and historical day!”

Lohan herself also commemorated the special day by posting a funny snap to Instagram of the scene where Aaron asks Cady what day it is.
