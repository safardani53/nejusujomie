Leandro Lo Pereira do Nascimento, is a multiple-time Brazilian jiu-jitsu world champion. He has also won BJJ events including the World Cup, Pan American championships and Brazilian National Jiu-Jitsu Championships. He held an unbeaten winning streak at the Copa Podio from 2011 to 2013. Lo earned his black belt under Cicero Costha at Cicero Costha Academy in São Paulo, Brazil. In 2015, he created the New School "NS" Brotherhood team. His ferocious guard passing and tenacious guard play have made Lo one of the "best pound for pound grapplers to have ever graced the mat."
