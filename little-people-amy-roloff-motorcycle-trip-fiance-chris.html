LITTLE People, Big World star Amy Roloff took off on an “epic motorcycle road trip” with fiancé Chris Marek to celebrate his birthday earlier this month, sharing photos from their adventure with fans.

Meanwhile, her ex-husband Matt spent the last couple of weeks renovating the pool at the family farm after Amy finally moved off the property.

On Saturday, the LPBW star, who has also been renovating her own new home with Chris, showed off her new biker babe look with a gallery of shots from her motorcycle trip.

Included was a sweet coupled-up shot of her an Chris with their helmets and sunglasses on and an open field behind them.

She captioned the collection: “Small towns (I wonder what they were like in their day) ), open range, fields, farms... a whole lot of area but very few people now. Beautiful country still”

In one of the photos, the mother of four pretended to be locked up inside of an old jail cart, while Chris posed in the doorway of a forgotten marshal’s office in another snap.

Just a few days ago, Amy posted another large slideshow, which included more smiling photos of the two of them in their motorcycle gear, as well as some shots with friends they met up with along the way.

She detailed her adventure, writing: “Another ‘annual’ awesome epic motorcycle trip (a week a go) with ♥️ Chris and friends. 

“I was excited and happy to ride behind him again and see more of Oregon and Idaho and the beauty of the NW. We rode on the back roads through eastern Oregon and Idaho to Sun Valley. Just beautiful. 

“Love traveling and seeing more of America. We stayed at Angela’s house (who rode w/ us to Idaho) and had a great time time riding the backroads there. 

The 55-year-old reality star, who spent some of her free time earlier this month with a girls' night in with some friends after a a day of filming, continued: “Thank you so much. Gracious hospitality. Great ride. 

“I loved it and hope to do and see more. Chris plans the whole route (big job) and it was fantastic. 

“We needed this trip and so glad we got to still make it happen. Love riding with my man”

The whole trip was to celebrate Chris’ birthday, which was on August 10, and Amy wrote him a sweet note on Instagram to commemorate his special day, telling him she’s “thrilled” to celebrate with her fiance for “years to come.”

While she was away, her ex-husband Matt proudly showed off his renovated pool after his ex-wife Amy finally left the family farm.

The 58-year-old patriarch displayed the impressive before-and-after transformation of the pool on the 32-acre property.

Amy used to live on the Roloff farm with Matt, with whom she shares four children - twin sons Jeremy and Zachary, 32, daughter Molly, 26, and son Jacob, 23.

After 30 years of marriage, she and Matt finalized their divorce in 2016, but the Roloff matriarch didn’t leave the family home until recently when she got a payout of $667,000 from Matt for her portion of the house.
