Melasma 

The model revealed that she had a skin condition called Melasma, which usually got worse during the summer.

In Melasma, a person has tan or dark skin discoloration, which may cause due to sun exposure, genetic predisposition, hormone changes, and skin irritation.

Hence, Edwards reached out to “The Skin Bar” and explained her problems to the owner and skin therapist, Samantha.

Initially, Madi was under the impression that the dark patches were caused due to sun damage. 

However, she later found out that she had developed Melasma because of her contraceptive pill, which caused a change in her hormones.

Samantha treated her Melasma with skin needling and Cosmelan peel. 

Besides that, the Instagram star takes care of her skin at home by using The Skin Bar’s Homecare kit daily, which prevents the Melasma from creeping back in.

As of now, she is melasma-free but will undergo skin needling again for collagen production.
