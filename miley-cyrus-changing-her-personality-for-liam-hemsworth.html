Changing Her Personality For Liam Hemsworth?

April 19, 2024
blog

It’s been two months since we first learned that Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth are back together, and while Miley has remained her usual bonkers self on social media, one tabloid is claiming she’s become a very different person at home.

"[Miley] has been cooking and cleaning for Liam, waiting on him hand and foot,” a source close to the couple tells Us Weekly, and while there’s certainly nothing wrong with the singer playing house like some sort of stoned June Cleaver, it’s her alleged reason for becoming domesticated that’s a bit unsettling:

“She wants to make sure she keeps him," says the insider.

As you probably know, Miley and Liam’s relationship has fizzled out once before, and this isn’t the first report of the 23-year-old changing her ways in order keep it from happening again.

There have even been rumors about Miley getting pregnant in order to "trap" Liam.  

While we seriously doubt she’d ever take it that far, we could see how having endured a painful breakup with the same guy once before might make Miley a bit nervous about the prospect of things ending badly again.

Unfortunately, the source claims that Miley’s even gone so far as to ditch her friends in order to keep Liam happy:

“She either doesn’t answer their calls or says she’s not going out," says the insider. "Liam never liked that she got crazy, so Miley’s making big changes. She doesn’t want to screw it up."

Still no official word on whether or not there’s any truth to the rumors of Miley and Liam getting engaged, but if she really is losing touch who she is because of this dude, we’re hoping he never puts a ring on it.
