NAOMI Watts can be seen stripping off for a raunchy sex scene in the new series of Twin Peaks.

The 48-year-old actress can be seen getting cosy with her on-screen husband, FBI agent Dale Cooper (played by Kyle MacLachlan).

In episode ten, Naomi goes naked in the scene as she straddles Kyle in bed.

With bed sheets protecting her modesty, the star can be seen in the throes of pleasure for the raunchy scenes.

Twin Peaks is a drama series created by novelist and screenwriter Mark Frost and surrealist director David Lynch.

It first aired in 1991 and followed the murder investigation of homecoming queen Laura Palmer.

Set in the fictional town of Twin Peaks, Washington, it ran for two seasons and was followed by a feature film in 1992 called Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me.

It’s supernatural effects, as well as the surreal and quirky characters, not to mention its unsettling tone and scenery made it an instant cult classic.

According to Frost’s new book – The Secret History Of Twin Peaks – after Dale Cooper’s investigation of the murder of Palmer, the FBI gets hold of a box containing “a huge dossier”.

The book reveals that a female FBI agent is tasked to analyse all the documents inside, and everything related to the strange town of Twin Peaks.
