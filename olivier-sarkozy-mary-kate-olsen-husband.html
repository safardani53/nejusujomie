Who is Mary-Kate Olsen’s ex-husband, Olivier Sarkozy?

Oliver was born Pierre Olivier Sárközy on May 26, 1969, in Paris.

He is a 54-year-old French banker.

Oliver has worked for several renowned corporations and organizations such as Dillon, Read & Co, UBS Investment Bank, The Carlyle Group LP, The TCW Group, and Credit Suisse First Boston.

He is a trustee of the University of St Andrews American Foundation Scholarship and serves as one of the members of the Board of Directors of BankUnited.

He has also gained media attention in the past for being the half-brother of former French president Nicolas Sarkozy.

When did Mary-Kate and Olivier get divorced?

After tying the knot on November 27, 2015, at a private residence in New York City, Mary-Kate later filed for divorce from Olivier on April 17, 2020.

Even though she filed for an emergency order to proceed despite the delays caused by the coronavirus pandemic on May 13 of that year, the order was denied a year later.

Nevertheless, their divorce was finalized on January 25, 2021.
