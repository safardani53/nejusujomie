Not very many artists are world-renowned by a single name but ask anyone who watched TV in the 80s, 90s, and 2000s. They for sure know the name Raven and you can check out photos of her, here.

Raven-Symoné has appeared in some of the most iconic TV shows of all time, including her own Disney Channel series, ‘That’s So Raven,’ which ran from 2003 to 2007. She currently stars in the spinoff series ‘Raven’s Home.’ She notably starred in Disney Channel Original Movies like ‘Zenon’ and ‘The Cheetah Girls’ films. Before her Disney Channel days, she got her start as Olivia Kendall on ‘The Cosby Show.’

As she grew up in the industry, she added titles like singer, producer, and talk show host to her resume, as well as out and proud activist in the LGBTQ community. From her big beginnings to the spotlight, to her recent surprise wedding to Miranda Maday, here are highlights over the years of Raven’s exciting life.
