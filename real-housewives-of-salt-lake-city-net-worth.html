Who is the richest housewife from the Real Housewives of Salt Lake City?

July 05, 2024
blog

EACH of the Real Housewives of Salt Lake City has settled down and are growing their fortunes in Utah, despite not necessarily having grown up there.

RHOSLC’s inaugural season debuted on November 11 on Bravo and stars Lisa Barlow, Jen Shah, Heather Gay, Meredith Marks, Whitney Rose, and Mary Cosby.

Who is the richest star of The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City?

Lisa Barlow and Mary Cosby top the list of wealthiest RHOSLC cast members with a net worth of $5 million each.

Barlow recently gave viewers a tour of her minimalist Utah home, admitting that she is a fan of "simple, very clean, long lines."

"I want everyone that comes in my home to feel like they're part of my house," she added. "And to feel cozy, comfortable."

Lisa is already no stranger to drama on the series.

In a clip for the second episode of the new Bravo series, Whitney gets emotional talking to her friend Heather Gay, and alleges Lisa "threatened to use rumors" against her.

The pair had had an argument over the behavior of Lisa's bartenders, and Whitney alleged: "After the party [Lisa] calls me immediately and she proceeded to tell me 'do not come after her entities,' then she threatened to use rumors that she had heard about me against me.

"Lisa accused me of being a swinger.

"In her mind, how could they possibly be that close as friends and not be swingers?"

How much are the housewives worth?

Lisa Barlow

Lisa Barlow is originally from New York and currently lives in Utah with her husband and business partner, John Barlow.

They run a tequila distillery and a marketing company called LUXE.

Lisa, who doesn't adhere to strict Mormon traditions and practices, labels herself a "Mormon 2.0."

According to her Bravo Bio, Lisa “seemingly has it all.” However, she “struggles with her quest for perfection when her unrealistic standards start impacting her friendships.”

She is reportedly worth $5million.

Mary Cosby

Mary Cosby is a business magnate who inherited her family’s empire of churches, restaurants, and other ventures.

Mary was reportedly forced to marry Rosemary's second husband, Bishop Robert C. Cosby, in order to assume her family's million-dollar fortune left behind by her grandma after her sudden heart attack death in 1997.

Her businesses have built her a $5 million net worth.

Heather Gay

Heather Gay, who was once Mormon royalty, co-owns a cosmetic medical practice called Beauty Lab and Laser.

She also co-hosts the Live Love Lab podcast.

The company has helped her build an estimated $1.7 million net worth.

Meredith Marks

Jewelry designer Meredith Marks already mixes in celebrity circles, as she makes trinkets for celebs from Rihanna to Hannah Brown.

Her designs are featured in Neiman Marcus stores.

She has also worked in real estate as well as invested in a health club.

She is worth an estimated $1 million.

Whitney Rose

Whitney Rose left the Mormon church around 2010 after falling in love with her boss, with whom she was having an affair.

The pair divorced their spouses and have been married for ten years.

She owns a skincare line called Iris and Beau and boasts almost 16,000 followers on Instagram.

Whitney is worth $3million according to Cheat Sheet.

Jen Shah

Jen is of Tongan and Hawaiian descent and grew up in Salt Lake City. 

Jen grew up Mormon but converted to Islam when she learned about the historical mistreatment of black people in her previous religion.
