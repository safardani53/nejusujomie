Five new and returning items at Trader Joes starting at $3 - from sips to soups

August 24, 2024
blog

THIS holiday season has great treats for shoppers at Trader Joe's as new and returning items hit the shelves already.

There are five items from Trader Joe's that are worth checking out — from soups to snacks, starting at $3.

TikTok influencer, taliaheskett, posted a video sharing five new and returning items at Trader Joe's.

In particular, there are some drinks, snacks, and soups that are coming back for this holiday season.

1. O' Nog Non-Dairy Oat Beverage

First up, the O' Nog Non-Dairy Oat Beverage is a classic egg nog beverage that's new to the stores.

The O’ Nog is a non-dairy version that calls on the power of oats to deliver the same creamy, satisfying, nutmeg-and-ginger profile, while still being vegan-friendly.

It is listed for only $2.99, according to a local store price viewed by The Sun.

But keep in mind that prices can vary by location.

2. Autumnal Harvest Soup

The Autumnal Harvest Soup is made with smooth butternut squash, semi-sweet pumpkin, and California tomatoes seasoned with earthy autumn herbs including rosemary and sage.

"If you guys are a fan of this pasta sauce I would definitely try out the soup," Talia said.

It is then finished with rich heavy cream, costing only $3.99.

This is only a seasonal item, so get it before it's gone.

3. Pumpkin Biqsue

Take advantage of the fall season while it lasts because Trader Joe's Pumpkin Bisque is also here for a limited time.

It is adjacent to the Autumnal Harvest soup and it is also $3.99.

The bisque is thick, rich, creamy.

It is made with pumpkin purée, heavy cream, honey and spices including cinnamon and nutmeg. 

4. Apple Caramels

Although Halloween is over, it doesn't mean candy can't be eaten.

"A popular candy they just got in are these Apple caramels," Talia said.

Trader Joe's Apple caramels are a new launch, and it is handmade with apple cider.

The price for this snack is $3.49.

5. Papadums Lentil and Chickpea crisps

"Returning are the Papadums lentil and chickpea crisps," Talia said.

These crisps are super thin, crunchy and flavorful.

This snack is only $2.69.
