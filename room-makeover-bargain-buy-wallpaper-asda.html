PANELLING is a very popular trend at the moment, but if you haven’t got a massive budget to get a professional to come and do it for you, don’t worry, you’ve come to the right place. 

If you fancy giving your home a glow-up but without splashing the cash, get inspired by this budget transformation.

Although the cost of living crisis continues to affect households up and down the country, this doesn’t mean that you can’t live in style.

Gemma Hunter, also known on TikTok as ‘thirtythree_sea’ took to the video sharing platform to show off her budget home transformation and we are very impressed. 

The Scottish, ‘nearly 40’ woman, spent just £12.95 on a roll of wallpaper and instantly gave her plain white room a refresh. 

In one of her recent videos, the DIY savvy woman shows off how she drastically transformed the corner of her room for just £12.95.

At the start of the video, we see the corner of Gemma’s room - it is very plain and white.

However, Gemma points a roll of wallpaper at the tired walls and just seconds later, she jumps to the finished result.

Gemma uses a wood slat wallpaper to upgrade her room and the transformation is absolutely stunning.
