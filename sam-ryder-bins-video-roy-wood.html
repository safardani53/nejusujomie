SAM RYDER’s festive season has become a lot dearer than usual – after he scrapped a video costing thousands.

However, mysterious circumstances led to the entire project being canned and a new video being hastily filmed.

That was just in time for today’s chart, when the track will battle Wham!’s Last Christmas for the No1 spot.

On Sunday, it was revealed Sam’s tune had risen from No12 to No2 and was just 800 copies behind the Eighties classic, which topped the charts last Friday. Quizzed about the video, Sam said: “We ain’t said anything, I can’t confirm or deny.

Christmas No1 contenders

THE race is on for Christmas No1 and a sackful of contenders have entered the race ahead of the big reveal next Friday.

They will face competition from The Pogues’ Fairytale Of New York following frontman Shane MacGowan’s death last month.

The song peaked at No2 in 1987 and last week shot back up the chart to No4. The band and his family have backed a campaign to get it to the top and 7” vinyl singles will be released on Tuesday for charity.

Last week’s No1, Last Christmas by Wham!, also stands a good chance, while bookmaker Coral has set odds for NoahKahan’s song Stick Season and Mariah Carey’s anthem All I Want For Christmas Is You, too.
