Smokers Club Festival 2022: How do I buy tickets?

WITH 2022 well underway many music festivals are announcing their events for the rest of the year.

Smokers Club Festival 2022 will have some of the biggest names in music headlining the event.

How can I buy tickets to Smokers Club Festival 2022?

The official Instagram account for The Smokers Club released the information about the festival on January 20, 2022.

Tickets for the rap music festival will be sold exclusively on The Smokers Club Festival website.

Fans can now sign up to obtain the presale code via their website.

"WE’RE BACK. APRIL 30. SO CAL," the festival wrote on Twitter.

The presale event will begin on January 24, 2022 at 10am PST.

Smokers Club Festival 2022 will be taking place in Southern California at the Glen Helen amphitheater.

How much do tickets cost for Smokers Club Festival 2022?

Tickets for the rap music festival are listed on their website are said to start from "$19.99 down."

Similar to the When We Were Young festival tickets, there are different prices for different tiers.

General admission tickets for the Smokers Club Festival start at $222.99 and go upwards to $252.99 for the fourth tier.

There is only one VIP package available and it is listed at $408.99.

In addition to admission to the event the single VIP package includes the following according to their website:

  • Performances all day at Glen Helen Amphitheater
  • Access to Pit Area
  • Food choices from regional and local vendors
  • Bars, concessions, official band merch, festival merch, free water stations, and more
  • Air-conditioned restrooms
  • Dedicated entry lane at main entrance
  • Charging stations
