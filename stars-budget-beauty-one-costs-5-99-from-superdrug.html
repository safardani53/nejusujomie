From Maya Jama to Michelle Keegan and Victoria Beckham, stars who love budget beauty and one cost

October 07, 2024
blog

AS millionaire model Abbey Clancy reveals she can’t live without her £3.99 Rimmel lip liner, Sarah Morton looks at High Street beauty buys the stars swear by...

MARGOT ROBBIE – Burt’s Bees tinted lip balm, Boots, £5.99: The Aussie actor doesn’t rely on super expensive makeup to look fantastic, though of course it helps that the Barbie star is naturally stunning.

Instead she swears by this cult favourite which leaves your lips feeling super nourished with a hint of colour.

“I don’t like wearing lipstick because I get it all over my face,” she admits. “So I wear a lip balm with a bit of stain, like Burt’s Bees.”

MAYA JAMA – Rimmel liquid eyeliner, Superdrug, £5.99:  Most of us would kill to look like Maya, but when it comes to her own beauty regime, the gorgeous Love Island presenter needs little more than this budget liquid eyeliner.

And, despite access to some of the most exclusive cosmetics in the world, Maya reckons this one is “the best”. “It’s really hard to find a good liner,” she explains.

“This one has a really good pointed brush-tip thing that you can easily swipe on in one application.

If you’re an eyeliner girl, I think it’s the best one.”

GIGI HADID – Maybelline lifter gloss, Boots, £8.99: AS every  supermodel knows, it’s important to keep those lips luscious – and Gigi makes sure of it with this nifty little gloss, which retails at less than £9.

Speaking of the bargain beauty buy, millionaire Gigi gushed: “It’s really cool because it has a lifting serum in it, so it really plumps your lips.”

One look at that gorgeous bee-stung mouth of hers should be enough to get it flying off the shelves.

MICHELLE KEEGAN - L’Oreal tattoo liquid eyeliner,  Amazon.co.uk, £5.43: She is back on screen in Netflix drama Fool Me Once and everyone is talking about Michelle again.

Most notably: How does she manage to look so good all of the time? The down-to-earth  star stays true to her roots  – she once worked on Selfridge’s make up counter – by hitting the High Street.

She said, “I really like High Street beauty brands. I swear by L’Oréal’s liquid eyeliners. They are so reasonable and are really long-lasting.”

VICTORIA BECKHAM – Weleda Skin Food, Holland and Barrett, £8.95: With her cosmetics range selling eye shadow for £56, you’d think Posh would stick to the expensive stuff when it comes to her own face.

However, the former Spice Girl is refreshingly with the rest of us when it comes to her skin care, opting for this cheap, but very effective moisturiser which is also favoured by songstress Rihanna.

“It keeps your skin so hydrated and moisturised,” Victoria raved. “It’s really, really thick and I love how it feels all over the body. It’s a really great product.”

KENDALL JENNER – L’oréal Paris liquid lipstick, Amazon.co.uk, £9.99: If you’ve been Keeping Up With Kendall’s beauty regime, you’ll know she adores this lippy, which can be sourced online for less than a tenner.

Let’s assume she’s not just saying it because she’s L’oreal’s ambassador. “It’s a liquid lipstick, and it’s so easy to wear, never smudges, and feels super hydrating – I love it,” she gushed.

It comes in a variety of different colours, and there’s no doubt supermodel Kendall has the lot. She can afford it, after all.
