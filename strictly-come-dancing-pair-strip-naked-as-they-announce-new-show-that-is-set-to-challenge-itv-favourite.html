STRICTLY Come Dancing's Anton Du Beke and Giovanni Pernice posed naked as they announced their new BBC show - and it's set to rival a popular ITV favourite.

Following their adventures earlier this year in Sicily, the dancing duo will packing their passports once again for Anton and Giovanni’s Adventures in Spain.

The BBC show will rival popular travel programme Gino, Gordon and Fred, which features TV chefs Gino D'Acampo, Gordon Ramsay and First Dates star Fred Sirieix.

Giovanni shared the special news by posting a snap of the pair in the nude, with only a newspaper to cover their modesty.

He wrote: "Happy bday to me (33) and what better way to celebrate than by sharing this news with you all!!

"Me and my best friend just got back from filming another adventure .. and this time it is in Spain !! Here is to us stopping to do some laundry.

It’s now Anton’s turn to showcase his special place as he introduces Giovanni to a host of delights that the southern European country has to offer.

Anton fell in love with Spain as a child, spending his school summer holidays with his Spanish family in the North of the country.

The first series, Anton and Giovanni’s Adventures in Sicily, is available to watch now via BBC iPlayer.
