TATE McRae has announced her THINK LATER Tour, which will take her on a global tour the next year.

The tour comes in support of her new album, Think Later, which is due to debut on November 18, 2023.

April 17 kicks off the tour with two performances in Dublin, and it will go on to London, Amsterdam, Berlin, Paris, Barcelona, and other cities.

The North American portion of McRae's tour will then begin on July 5th with a hometown event in Calgary, followed by performances in Los Angeles, Austin, Toronto, Nashville, and New York.

You were able to begin to participate in this presale today, Tuesday, November 7th at 10 am local venue time—9 am for anyone in New York—if you own an American Express card. To make a purchase, simply choose a Tate McRae tour date and pay with your AMEX card.

Tickets are going for as low as $54 and as high as $304.

  • Sophomore album coming in December

    Tate McRae will be debuting her second album on December 8, only weeks prior to her SNL performance gig.

    “This album means sooo much to me and I can’t believe it’s so close to coming out. Writing this whole thing was one of the most stressful, exciting, nerve racking, and fun things I’ve ever gone through,” McRae wrote on Instagram.

    “For the first time in my life I lived this year a little less with my head and a little more with my intuition – and I rlly hope u guys can feel that through the music. Through the biggest highs of my life to the lowest of lows – I feel so happy that it is now gonna be yours <3 here’s to 20 years old and figuring [out] who the f**k i am haha.”

  • More to know about Tate

    Tate launched her dance-focused YouTube channel, Create With Tate, after the program, but four weeks into the account, she posted a song.

    Since then, the song "One Day" has received well over 40 million views.

    But with around 900 million Spotify plays, "You Broke Me First" became her first hit to peak at number one in the US and in the top three in the UK.

    The up-and-coming musician is now ranked 114th in the world by Spotify, which has 25 million monthly listeners.

    Her Spotify bio says: "hi i'm tate. i write songs because they're all the things i never got to say."

  • Everything to know about Tate McRae

    Tate McRae is a 20-year-old singer, composer, and dancer from Canada.

    She was raised in Calgary by her mother, a dance instructor, brother, Tucker, and father, a lawyer.

    She began dancing at the young age of six, went to the esteemed Alberta Ballet in Canada, and at the age of eleven, she took home a silver medal at the 2015 Youth America Grand Prix.

    She went to the 13-year-old season auditions of the American television program So You Think You Can Dance, where the judges were Jason Derulo, Paula Abdul, and Nigel Lythgoe.
