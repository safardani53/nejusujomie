TEEN Mom Amber Portwood has angered fans over her outburst toward her co-star Ashley Jones as they're pleading for the star to be fired.

Things have gotten even uglier between the Teen Mom: The Next Chapter cast following their explosive feud at the reunion.

Over the weekend, Ashley, 25, leaked a phone dispute between her and Amber, 32, after their fallout at the special.

Much of the conversation, shared on Ashley's Instagram Stories, was Amber shouting expletives through the phone as Ashley recorded the tirade.

Ashley repeatedly tried to brush off her co-star's words, telling her she "didn't care" about what she had to say.

But when Amber's rant continued, she eventually asked: "What do you want from me?"

FANS' REACTIONS

The video surfaced on an online thread, where fans begged MTV to cut ties with Amber for her behavior.

"She 100% should be kicked off," one person proposed.

"Amber can abuse other cast members and not get fired- She must have something on MTV because nobody likes her, and she still never gets fired…" another complained.

EXPLOSIVE FEUD

The incident that prompted Amber's rant occurred as the ladies left the Teen Mom Family Reunion set.

The Ashleys Reality Roundup reported on two incidents that took place - one involving Ashley and her co-star Briana DeJesus, 28 - and the other between Ashley and Amber.

A source told the outlet: "At the end of filming, all the girls other than Ashley got into two vans to leave the set. They passed Ashley on their way out and she realized who was in the vans. That's when she began doing her signature twerking moves at the second van."

The outlet did not reveal that Amber was upset by the move, but an insider said: "A lot of the girls found it super disrespectful and it really angered one girl in particular. Someone who had not had any previous disagreements with Ashley and had previously really liked her."

It was only once Ashley leaked the call between herself and Amber that it became clear who the insider was talking about.

LEGAL ISSUES

Fans have watched Amber's complicated past play out on screen, though she has appeared to turn her life around in the latest season.

In 2010, the TV star faced domestic abuse charges when she hit her ex-partner and baby daddy, Gary Shirley, while Teen Mom cameras were rolling.

Gary was given full custody of their daughter, Leah, 13, when she spent 17 months in jail for violating her drug probation requirements in 2012.

She later found herself in more legal trouble after she was arrested for domestic violence against her now ex-Andrew Glennon in July 2019.

The former partners are parents to a 4-year-old son, James.

Amber lost custody of James in July 2022 after a heated three-year court battle with Andrew, 38.

Earlier this month, The U.S. Sun confirmed that Andrew and James moved 2,000 miles away from Amber's Indiana home at his mother's $5 million estate in Malibu.

Last week, Amber urged fans to be kind since the court ruling in an Instagram post.
