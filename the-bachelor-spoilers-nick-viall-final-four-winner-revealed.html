Nick Viall Final Four, WINNER Revealed!?

June 16, 2024
blog

Although the final rose ceremony of Season 21 hasn’t even been filmed yet, The Bachelor spoilers are already rolling in and then some.

Raise your hand if you’re surprised.

That’s right, per The Bachelor spoilers guru and legendary blogger Reality Steve, the members of Nick Viall’s final four are revealed!

Along with the WINNER! Already!

As you’re probably heard by now, Nick is back for a third (or we suppose fourth, if you include Bachelor in Paradise) crack at TV love.

After being rejected by Andi Dorfman and Kaitlyn Bristowe on back-to-back The Bachelorette finales, he’s handing out the roses this time!

No rejection of Nick at the final rose ceremony this time, at least … although we suppose he could theoretically get turned down again. 

Which would be unlikely, but pretty epic TV. Just saying.

Anyway, the premiere won’t air until next YEAR (albeit the second day of next year, which isn’t THAT far away), but Steve did it again.

Right now, Nick is filming his overnight dates, which means Viall is down to just three women (all of whom he will probably sleep with).

Anyway, who are his final four women this season?

1. Rachel Lindsay from Dallas, Texas, who would be the first African-American to ever make the final four if The Bachelor spoilers are true.

2. Corinne Olympios of Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

3. Raven Gates from Hoxie, Arkansas.

4. Vanessa Grimaldi of Montreal, Canada.

The order in which The Bachelor eliminates three of the four ladies listed about remains to be seen, but those are Nick’s chosen four.

Like Ben Higgins’ season, when spoilers indicated that the final pick was a “done deal” even before filming wrapped, is that the case here?

Oh yes, according to the ultimate insider. Of Rachel Lindsay, Raven Gates, Vanessa Grimaldi, and Corinne Olympios, he has a clear favorite.

Montreal special education teacher and actress Vanessa Grimaldi, 29, was the obvious final pick early on, and "definitely" will prevail.

Or so we’re told.

“What I can tell you from everything I’m hearing so far is this: Vanessa is going to win,” Steve writes, and the man is never wrong.

Okay, he has been sometimes, but when you look at the entire body of work over the last 6-7 years, he’s been pretty incredible.

“I told you midway through Ben’s season I was hearing it was a done deal with Lauren," he adds, "and that ended up panning out."

Indeed it did.

Moreover, the spoilers titan notes that he "told you last season it was basically the JoJo and Jordan show, and that ended up panning out as well."

Truth.

Now, for the upcoming iteration, he’s all but guaranteeing that this is over and done with again, before the first girl steps out of a limo.

"From what I’ve been told," Steve writes on his blog, "Nick is going to pick Vanessa in the end [and I] can’t say I’m the least bit surprised.”

What is it about Vanessa that gives Nick Viall that magical feeling? Will this finally be the year for the unlucky-in-love franchise veteran?

Time will tell.
