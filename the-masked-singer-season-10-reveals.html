All of The Masked Singer Season 10 Reveals

March 23, 2024
blog

'The Masked Singer' Season 10 reveals: a handy list of everyone who has been unmasked on the most recent season of our favorite singing show.

Spoiler alert: This article contains spoilers for Season 10 of The Masked Singer.

Season 10 of The Masked Singer kicked off on Sept. 26, 2023, and with it came new masks. As creative and over-the-top as the costumes are, the identities of the celebrities underneath will remain a mystery until they're eliminated and unmasked.

We're keeping a running list of all the reveals this season. Be sure to check back after each episode to see who has been unmasked! We've got all of the details for you.

Anonymouse — Demi Lovato

Anonymouse was revealed as part of the Season 10 kickoff special on Sept. 10, 2023. She sang Heart's "What About Love" and gave several clues, including the fact that she has been on "one special list times 100," she started her career young, and had some connection to Nick Jonas.

Nicole Scherzinger was able to put the clues together and correctly guess that Anonymouse was Demi Lovato.

Article continues below advertisement

Rubber Ducky — Anthony Anderson

Rubber Ducky was revealed during the official Season 10 premiere on Sept. 27, 2023. He sang "Come on Eileen" by Dexys Midnight Runners and gave several clues, including the fact that his "family is full of stars," he tried stand-up comedy and had some connection to Leonardo DiCaprio.

Ken Jeong was able to put the clues together and correctly guess that Rubber Ducky was Anthony Anderson.

Article continues below advertisement

Diver — Tom Sandoval

Diver was revealed on Oct. 4, 2023, as Tom Sandoval. The Vanderpump Rules star spoke about being judged right before his unmasking. He has received a ton of backlash lately for his infidelity, so it appears that under the mask he was able to dodge all the negativity he has been swamped with.

Ken Jeong was the only one to put the clues together correctly for the final guess.

Pickle — Michael Rapaport

Pickle was a strong contender on the game for this season, but he sadly didn't win. However, we now know his real identity: comedian and actor Michael Rapaport. Nick Cannon laughed at the judges as they didn't answer correctly a single time and confessed that he figured it out since he's friends with Michael.

Royal Hen — Billie Jean King

The Royal Hen is none other than trailblazing tennis icon Billie Jean King! The legendary athlete and feminist pioneer lost out to the Hawk but more importantly, she got to sing the Elton John classic Philadelphia Freedom, a song he wrote in honor of King back in the '70s. Though she is not going to take home that coveted Masked Singer mask trophy, we think she'll be OK seeing as she won everything else.

Hawk — Tyler Posey

The mighty Hawk fell and it was Tyler Posey, the dreamy star of the Teen Wolf TV show. He plays the part that Michael J. Fox famously played in the '80s movie classic the show is based on.

Hibiscus — Countess Luann de Lesseps

The Hibiscus has wilted on the vine, but who is inside is still fabulous. We're talking about Countess Luann de Lesseps, the Real Housewives of New York star. She is apparently a cabaret singer now. Alas, that experience didn't give her enough advantage to beat the Anteater. She even sang the RHONY classic, "Money Can't Buy You Class." Indeed it can't.

Cuddle Monster — Metta World Peace

After the cutest showdown against the Anteater, Cuddle Monster was eliminated — and unmasked to be former NBA star Metta World Peace, formerly known as Ron Artest.

“I had a fun time singing that song,” he told Variety of his New Kids on the Block song choice. “And I also had a fun time learning how to sing and how to bring out the best for my horrible vocals.”

S'More — Ashley Parker Angel

The S'More was Ashley Parker Angel, best known for being a member of the 2000s boy band O-Town. Jenny McCarthy was the only judge to guess his identity correctly.
