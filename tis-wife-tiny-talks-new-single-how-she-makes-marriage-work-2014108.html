T.I.'s Wife Tiny Talks New Single, How She Makes Marriage Work

March 29, 2024
blog

Major! Clifford “T.I.” Harris‘ wife Tameka “Tiny” Harris revealed to Us Weekly in an interview with Entertainment Director Ian Drew everything from why she released a new single to how her marriage to the rapper is holding out.

“I recorded this song about a year ago,” Tiny tells Us of her new hit single “What The F@#k You Gon Do?” “I went into the studio and just wanted to write about some things I was feeling and … it was just for me and my friends to hear and then my team got the song and said, ‘Yo. We are putting this song out’ … And they talked me into it,” she added. 

When it comes to “some things I was feeling,” The Family Hustle reality star admits her marriage—which has been under scrutiny the past months—to T.I. is “really good now.”

“We are really good now,” Tiny tells Us. “We are just taking it one day at a time. Everything is looking good.”
