VANESSA Hudgens, Lily Collins, Chelsea Handler and more stars shined bright on the MTV Movie & TV Awards: Greatest Of All Time red carpet.

The special will honor film and television's biggest moments from the 1980s through today.

Awards show host, Vanessa Hudgens, struck a strong pose in a black strapless mini dress and gold statement necklace.

The actress also wore her hair in a bold, straight bob hairstyle.

Emily in Paris star, Lily Collins, turned out for the event in a skintight black, latex midi dress.

She looked over her shoulder as she posed for photographers.

Comedienne Chelsea Handler is paying tribute to the film and television industry in a flowy black, velvet dress with satin floral accents.

Because the awards are showcasing the "Greatest Of All Time," moments in TV and movies, a goat was chosen as the show's mascot.

So it also got an opportunity to walk the red carpet.

Singer and actress Sabrina Carpenter sparkled in a black see-through mesh rhinestone dress.

She wore her long, golden blonde locks down and straight and accessorized with diamond earrings.

Dancing With the Stars alum Derek Hough rocked black leather pants, a red paisley shirt and a brown suede code with fringe.
