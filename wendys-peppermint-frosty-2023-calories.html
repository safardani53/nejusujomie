Wendy's Peppermint Frosty: How long will it be available?

May 09, 2024
blog

WENDY'S is celebrating the holidays with the return of its Peppermint Frosty.

Now, customers want to know more about the festive dessert item and how long they have to enjoy the sweet treat.

What is Wendy's Peppermint Frosty?

Wendy's Peppermint Frosty is a seasonal item that has returned to the fast food chain's menu.

According to the company's website, it "has the delectable, creamy Frosty texture you know and love with bursts of fresh peppermint in every bite."

It also states: "The latest sweet treat is Wendy’s twist on a beloved flavor that will transport you to a world of holiday cheer."

How long will the Peppermint Frosty be available?

The Peppermint Frosty returned on November 14, 2023, and will be available in four sizes for a limited time.

Customers can purchase the seasonal treat throughout the holiday season.

It is expected to stop being sold sometime in early 2024.

In a November 14 news release, Wendy's also announced the launch of the new Peppermint Frosty Cream Cold Brew, which was inspired by the Peppermint Frosty and "features peppermint syrup swirled in smooth cold-brewed coffee with classic Frosty creamer over ice.

"The latest winter-inspired flavor joins Wendy's year-round chocolate, vanilla, and caramel flavor options."

Until November 19, 2023, the restaurant chain offered customers a free small Peppermint Frosty or Peppermint Frosty Cream Cold Brew to "celebrate" the holiday menu items.

Lindsay Radkoski, the US chief marketing officer for The Wendy's Company, said: "There is no better way to share holiday cheer with our fans than giving the gift of a free Frosty or Frosty Cream Cold Brew.

"When we say Free, we mean it!  This offer is no purchase required so we can truly surprise and delight our fans during the holidays with a sweet treat and even sweeter offer available in the Wendy's app."

How many calories are in a Peppermint Frosty?

The Peppermint Frosty's calorie count depends on its size.

According to Wendy's online nutrition information, the calorie count for the festive treat is, by size:

  • Jr. Peppermint Frosty - 220 calories
  • Small Peppermint Frosty - 340 calories
  • Medium Peppermint Frosty - 430 calories
  • Large Peppermint Frosty - 560 calories

When was the Peppermint Frosty first released?

The Peppermint Frosty was first released in November 2022.

As CNN Business reported at the time, its launch marked the first time the fast food chain included a holiday-themed Frosty within its menu.

The outlet reported that the minty flavor replaced the Strawberry Frosty that was released during the summer of 2022 and "sold so well that the company accelerated its plans for another new Frosty flavor."

Wendy’s chief marketing officer Carl Loredo said in an exclusive interview with CNN Business: "Folks enjoyed the Strawberry Frosty enough that we knew it was important to get after another flavor and one that would tie well with the holidays.

"Peppermint is amazing, and it’s a great way to ring in the holiday season."
