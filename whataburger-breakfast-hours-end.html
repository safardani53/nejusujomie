What time does Whataburger stop serving breakfast?

May 20, 2024
blog

WHATABURGER is a popular fast-food chain that is known for its hamburgers.

Along with burgers, the chain also serves breakfast, but only for a limited time throughout the day.

What time does Whataburger stop serving breakfast?

Whataburger began serving breakfast in 1979 and was among one of the first fast-food chains to do so.

However, the breakfast menu is only available for 12 hours.

Whataburger's breakfast menu begins at 11pm and ends at 11am.

They are known as one of the only chains to offer a late-night breakfast menu, as competitors often wait until 5am or later to start their breakfast service.

What does Whataburger serve for breakfast?

While Whataburger offers several breakfast options, the company is arguably most famous for the Breakfast On A Bun sandwich and Taquitos.

The Breakfast On A Bun was one of Whataburger's first breakfast items and is still enjoyed today.

more from fast food

It comes with egg, cheese, and either sausage or bacon.

The Taquitos are also customizable and come with egg, cheese, and either bacon, potatoes, or sausage.

Other popular breakfast items include:

  • Biscuit Sandwich
  • Pancake Platter
  • Breakfast Platter
  • Honey Butter Chicken Biscuit
  • Egg Sandwich
  • Egg and Cheese Biscuit
  • Sausage Biscuit

In July 2022, the company brought back its famous Breakfast Burger for a limited time.

The burger comes with a beef patty, eggs, hash brown sticks, cheese, bacon, and a creamy pepper sauce.

“The Breakfast Burger is one of the most popular limited-time breakfast offers we’ve ever introduced,” Rich Scheffler, the Whataburger senior vice president and chief marketing officer, said in a news release, via KSAT.

“We’re excited to answer the call for its return and share this special burger with new fans.”

The Breakfast Burger is available while supplies last.

Who owns Whataburger?

Whataburger was founded by Harmon Dobson and Paul Burton in Corpus Christi, Texas, in 1950.

In 2019, the founder's family decided to sell a majority interest in the company to BDT Capital, which is now the owner of Whataburger.

However, Tom, Lynne and Hugh Dobson, who are the children of founder Harmon Dobson, remain minority stakeholders.

"This is both exciting and bittersweet for the Dobson family. Whataburger has been the heart and soul of our family legacy for nearly 70 years, but we feel really good about the partnership with BDT," Tom Dobson said at the time, via Newsweek.
