Where is Annabelle Doll now?

February 12, 2024
blog

ANNABELLE Doll is a terrifying figure for those who have watched the Conjuring horror movies.

The creepy porcelain character is said to be inhabited by a demon. Here's all about the frightening toy...

Who is Annabelle Doll?

Annabelle Doll will be a familiar figure to those who have watched The Conjuring movies.

The doll has been described as a "terrifying porcelain doll that is disfigured and immediately menacing".

The doll is a recurring antagonist in the Conjuring movie franchise.

Annabelle also has movies in her own name, including Annabelle (2014), Annabelle: Creation (2017), and Annabelle Comes Home (2019).

In the original conjuring movie, it is explained that a demon became attached to the doll.

The background to the doll is further explored in the prequel movie, named Annabelle.

In Annabelle, husband John Form gives the doll to his pregnant wife Mia as a present.

The family are haunted whenever they're in the presence of the doll.

For those unfamiliar with The Conjuring films, the movies are a dramatized version of of the 'real-life cases' of paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren.

Warren has spoken about the 'real' story behind Anabelle Doll.

Unlike the porcelain doll seen in the movies, the real Annabelle doll was a normal looking Raggedy Ann Doll.

Where is Annabelle Doll now?

Annabelle Doll is now kept in Ed and Lorraine Warrens' Occult Museum in Monroe, Connecticut.

According to the real-life Warrens, a student nurse was given the doll in 1970. 

After a few strange incidents with the doll, a psychic medium told the nurse that the doll was inhabited by the spirit of a dead girl named "Annabelle".

Ed and Lorraine Warren were called to investigate, and deemed that the doll was indeed demonically possessed.
