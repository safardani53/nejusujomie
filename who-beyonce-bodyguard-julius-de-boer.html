Who is Beyonc's bodyguard Julius de Boer?

March 03, 2024
blog

JULIUS de Boer has worked with Beyoncé for 15 years.

Now, fans want to know more about the star entertainer's bodyguard.

Who is Beyoncé's bodyguard, Julius de Boer?

Julius de Boer is Beyoncé's longtime bodyguard who has served as the CEO of the security company, DeBoer Security Global, since January 2014.

Distractify states that according to his company's website, Julius grew up in Amsterdam and earned a degree from the University of Amsterdam. However, it seems that his website no longer exists.

According to Page Six, his degree is in psychology, and he started his business when he was 24 years old.

As per his LinkedIn profile, Julius is based in New York City, and his company operates in New York, London, and Dubai.

It states: "DEBOER SECURITY GLOBAL is unlike any other security company. With a passion and commitment for getting things right first time, we always go the extra mile to give you peace of mind that you will be at your safest and most secure with us.

"We are a Global network of experienced former law enforcement, military, and security professionals who have honed their skills conducting successful protective operations in multiple countries."

Julius' LinkedIn page also lists the company's services, which include:

  • Dignitary and Executive Protection
  • Intelligence Capabilities
  • Risk Assessment and Crisis Management
  • Social Media Monitoring
  • Worldwide Transportation

Prior to his role at DeBoer Security Global, Julius worked as a team leader for Nuon from 2002 to 2005.

According to Variety, he speaks five languages, though his LinkedIn page only lists four, including Dutch, English, French, and German.

In April 2011, Julius spoke with the publication about being a bodyguard, saying: "Adaptability, in physical and psychological terms, is the main characteristic that you must possess.

"It is much more than being a human shield to your customer. You should always think three steps ahead, follow a schedule, and call ahead to destinations and so on."

When did he start working for Beyoncé?

According to Distractify, Julius began working for the singing sensation in 2008 and "has been present for pretty much all" of the "iconic Beyoncé moments" that have taken place throughout the years.

The outlet states that he has been there "during all of her daily forays into the world," her concerts, and her live appearances.

It also reports that Julius was present when Beyoncé gave birth to her kids and was "even clearly present in the footage of Solange Knowles famously hitting Jay-Z in an elevator after the Met Gala."

What other celebrities has Julius de Boer worked with?

According to Essence, Julius has also worked with Beyoncé's husband, rapper Jay-Z.

At this time, information on additional celebrities the bodyguard has worked with is unknown.
