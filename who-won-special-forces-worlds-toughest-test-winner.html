Who won Special Forces: World's Toughest Test?

July 18, 2024
blog

FOX'S newest reality series Special Forces: World's Toughest Test premiered in January 2023 and has already crowned the first winners.

The show strips celebrities of their lavish lifestyles to see if they'll survive a 10-day Special Ops challenge in the middle of the Jordanian desert.

Who won Special Forces: World's Toughest Test?

During Season 1 of Special Forces: World's Toughest Test, fans saw 16 celebrities endure demanding training exercises that were led by an elite team of ex-Special Forces operatives.

The process tested their physical, mental, and emotional resilience, and ultimately only two of them were able to go all the way.

The season 1 winners were The Bachelorette and Dancing with the Stars alum Hannah Brown and former professional soccer player Carli Lloyd.

“It was such a taxing last day of all the interrogation and torture,” Hannah said during an interview with Hollywood Life.

“I’m saying torture, but it really kind of felt like that. I was just really shocked that it was actually over and that we had passed. I was also just really proud.

"This was something that was so out of my element, and I just went for it and tried my absolute best. To be able to accomplish that was something that kind of surprised me. I was really just honored to be also standing there with Carli.”

Carli added: “The last 24 hours were absolute torture. I don’t even know the place that I went to, but I went to a deep dark place and just tried to think of happy thoughts knowing that I was almost at the finish line. But I just kept telling myself I just didn’t want to quit. That was my main goal.

"I couldn’t bring myself to quit. Whatever I had to do to get through it, I was going to do so. It was extremely rewarding, and I felt incredibly proud and just pushed my limits to a mental capacity that I really didn’t know I had in me.
